## Design Context

### Users
Brands, startups, and companies looking for a premium, high-end digital identity. They value meticulous craft, immersive experiences, and state-of-the-art technology. The goal is to establish trust and awe through visual excellence.

### Brand Personality
- **Meticulous**: Every pixel, animation, and transition is intentional and refined.
- **Immersive**: Using horizontal scrolling and GSAP animations to create a cinematic journey.
- **Bold**: A strong, dark aesthetic with vibrant purple accents that stand out.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Inspired by Resend.com - sleek, dark, minimal yet technically sophisticated.
- **Key Features**: Black backgrounds (#000000), white typography, purple accents (#965EC7).
- **Themes**: Glassmorphism, subtle micro-animations, cinematic loaders, and Apple-inspired UI elements (e.g., refined CTAs).

### Design Principles
1. **Motion as Story**: Every animation should guide the user or provide delightful feedback, never distract.
2. **Contrast is King**: Maintain high visual hierarchy through sharp contrast between dark backgrounds and sharp, clean typography.
3. **Glass & Light**: Use subtle gradients and glassmorphism to add depth and a sense of premium "tactility" to the digital interface.
4. **Cinematic Scaling**: Elements should feel grand and immersive, often scaling or transitioning with fluid motion.
